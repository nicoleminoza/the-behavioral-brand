The Move · 03
The name, held in motion.
‘Leeum’ in severely distorted variable type forms a hypnotic half-crescent. At a glance, an abstract object; with attention, the letterforms. In motion it mimics a rolling tube, a shifting beacon, a colliding system of lines.
